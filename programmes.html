B.TECH COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING (ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE)

A dedicated program within the Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences is committed to pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence (AI). This program is centered on multiple facets of AI, including education, research, and application development. Its main goal is to lead innovative research across various AI domains such as computer vision, machine learning, and natural language processing. Within this program, students strive to enhance theoretical models while also creating practical AI solutions. This program offers courses designed to equip students with the understanding and abilities needed to engage, implement, and pioneer in the realm of AI.

DURATION: 4 YEARS

Infrastructure

Artificial intelligence stands as the foremost sought-after expertise in the business world, prompting Karunya to forge a partnership with Nvidia, a trailblazer in AI computer technology. B.TECH COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING (ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND MACHINE LEARNING)

This program aims to equip students with a solid understanding of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) principles and practices, along with practical skills to address real-world challenges. Throughout the program, students will delve into various AI and ML domains such as computer vision, natural language processing, robotics, and deep learning. They will gain proficiency in ML algorithms and techniques through hands-on experiences in labs and projects. By the end of the program, students will possess comprehensive knowledge and practical abilities in AI and ML, empowering them to pursue careers in diverse fields like software development and data analysis. They will be well-prepared to tackle complex problems and contribute to the continual progress of AI and ML technologies.

DURATION: 4 YEARS (FULL TIME)
