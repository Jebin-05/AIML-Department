SUSE Centre of Excellence for Cloud Computing

SUSE Centre of Excellence for Cloud Computing

The Division of Computer Science and Engineering signed a Memorandum of Agreement with the SUSE Academic Program, SUSE Software Solutions, Ireland, on 29th November 2019.

This initiative builds upon the division's earlier collaboration with Novell in 2010, through which the SUSE Certified Administrator (SCA) program in Enterprise Linux was first introduced. In 2014, Novell began supporting the M.Tech CSE program, leading to the creation of the SUSE Centre of Excellence for Cloud Computing at Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences.

Key Benefits of the Strategic Partnership

  • Certification training in Linux by SUSE Certified faculty/staff
  • Curriculum-aligned with industry trends for B.Tech and M.Tech students
  • Access to the online SUSE Academy for students and faculty
  • Use of SUSE products for lab/educational purposes
  • Access to training materials, development tools, support forums, and knowledge base
  • Support for branding, signage, and Coimbatore-based partner training sessions

Computing Security Research Lab

Computing Security Research Lab (CSR Lab)

The Computing Security Research Lab (CSR) is a dedicated advanced research centre within the Department of Computer Science and Engineering. It serves as a hub for students, scholars, and faculty to explore key domains including:

  • Network Security
  • Cloud Security
  • Big Data & IoT Security
  • Software Defined Networks (SDN)

Core Activities

  • Workshops & Seminars on computing security
  • Research Publications & Project Development
  • Infrastructure Enhancement
  • Curriculum Support for Core Security Labs

Industry Tie-Up

The CSR Lab has partnered with EC Council Academia (Partner ID: EACD17124) to offer cutting-edge certifications and resources for cyber security education.

Technical Events

Regular hands-on workshops and seminars are conducted by industry-trained professionals on topics such as:

  • Cyber Forensics
  • Ethical Hacking
  • Open-source Intelligence
  • Deep Learning for Cyber Security

Research & Publications

The lab is a key contributor to the Computing Security Research Group of the department. Since 2014, numerous publications in Scopus-indexed journals and conferences have originated from this lab's research work.
