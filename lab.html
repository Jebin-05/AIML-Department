IoT Centre for Artificial Intelligence

The Internet of Things (IoT) Lab provides an excellent infrastructure for young IT minds to nurture innovative ideas and solve real-world problems aligned with societal needs. It is an ideal learning space built around a hands-on, active environment.

The primary objective of this lab is to enable students to build smart and intelligent IoT solutions. The lab is well-equipped with tools and devices to provide immersive real-world experiences focusing on:

  • Data ingestion & acquisition
  • Machine Learning
  • Data visualization
  • Mobility and smart integration

Software Product Development Lab

The Software Product Development Lab of the Division of Computer Science and Engineering was established with the clear objective of encouraging students to develop software as products by following established software engineering principles.

Students form teams and are actively involved in developing innovative software solutions. This lab offers an environment for product development, group collaboration, technology exploration, self-learning, and project showcases.
